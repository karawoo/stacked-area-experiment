About this task

By completing this HIT, you will be participating in a research project. We are investigating how people interpret data visualizations. Your participation in this research is voluntary. You can return the task at any point by clicking “Return” and your data will not be saved or analyzed. If you complete the HIT, we will not retain your worker ID or any other identifying information with your answers. The data we collect will be published with the results of the study. You can contact the researcher Kara Woo with questions at karawoo@uw.edu.

You may complete as many HITs in this batch as you would like.
