Instructions

Please look at each of the charts below and determine which segment of the chart is smaller at the point where the chart is marked. Then, provide a quick visual judgment of what percentage the smaller segment is of the larger segment. Only consider the sizes of each segment at the point where the chart is marked. You can imagine drawing a vertical line through this point and comparing the segments to determine which is longer. However, do not try to make precise measurements, either mentally or with a physical object such as a pencil or your finger. We are interested in how well people make these judgments spontaneously and without the help of external aids. We will check that your responses are reasonable by comparing to other workers' responses for the same chart.
